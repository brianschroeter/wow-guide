## 6. Druid Tips (If You Go That Route) üêª

Druid is the **Swiss Army Knife** class - you can literally do everything:

| Spec | Role | Playstyle |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Guardian** | Tank | Big bear, take hits, lead dungeons |
| **Feral** | Melee DPS | Cat form, bleeds and combos, rogue-like |
| **Balance** | Ranged DPS | Moonkin/Boomie, lasers and dots |
| **Restoration** | Healer | HoTs (heal over time), chill healing style |

### Druid Leveling Perks
- **Travel Form** - Instant cast mount while leveling. Huge quality of life.
- **Flight Form** - Once you unlock flying, you get instant-cast flight. No mounting animation.
- **Stealth** - Cat form has stealth. Skip mobs you don't want to fight.
- **Self-Healing** - Every spec can off-heal in a pinch.

### Don't Stress About Spec
You can swap specs anytime once you unlock them (by level 10). Try them all. Zygor works with any spec.
